Hello, My name is Reagan Rogers and I am an aspiring Software Engineer. I am currently attending the Ohio State University Fullstack Coding Bootcamp in Columbus, Ohio where I reside. I had a first career as a marketing and sales professional but was laid off amid the global pandemic. I realized job security and broad range of skills were essential to surviving this era of grave uncertainty. During my unemployment, I researched new career paths that would create more job security and allow me to accumulate wealth. It was then that I fell in love with coding. Stretches of hours can go by before I realize just how much time I have spent at the computer building, editing, and growing my skill set. I knew coding was a true hobby I could monetize into a successful career path. I am excited to see where this journey takes me, especially as a woman of color in an male dominated field, as i pivot into my second career. In my spare time, I love to travel, spend time with friends, family, my furbaby Momo, and paint.
